Art@ogoma


I began my professional artistic journey back in 2013 during my highschool studies. Since then, I have participated in a number of collaborative projects and have exhibited my work worldwide. Unlike many of my counterparts, I am primarily self-taught and have my own approach to - and definition of - art. I purely deal in pencil art work, work-referencing photos provided by my clients and deliver within a maximum of 3 working days.
